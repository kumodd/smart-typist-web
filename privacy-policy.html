Privacy-First Commitment: Smart Typist is built with your privacy as the top priority. We do not store what you type, sell your data, or track your keystrokes. This policy explains transparently what limited data we do collect and why.
📊
Section 1

Information We Collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to provide and improve Smart Typist. Here is exactly what we collect and why:

1.1 What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your keystrokes or typed text (we never log what you type)
  • Passwords, credit card numbers, or any sensitive personal data
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Contact lists, messages, or media files
  • Personally identifiable information without your explicit consent

1.2 What We May Collect

  • Device information: Android version, device model (to fix compatibility issues)
  • App usage stats: Feature usage counts, crash reports (anonymized)
  • Subscription information: Purchase status via Google Play (not payment details)
  • Email address: Only if you subscribe to our newsletter or contact support
⚙️
Section 2

How We Use Your Information

Any information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Smart Typist keyboard app
  • To process AI text transformations you explicitly request
  • To manage your subscription and unlock premium features
  • To fix bugs and improve app performance using crash reports
  • To respond to your support requests and inquiries
  • To send product updates if you have subscribed (you can unsubscribe anytime)
We never use your data for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties.
🤝
Section 3

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share limited, anonymized data in these situations:

  • Service providers: Trusted providers like Google Play Billing (for subscriptions), Firebase (for crash analytics), and AI API providers for text processing — all under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal compliance: When required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of Smart Typist and its users
  • Business transfer: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your information may transfer, subject to the same privacy protections
🛡️
Section 4

Data Security

We take data security seriously and employ industry-standard protections:

  • Encryption in transit: All data sent to our AI providers is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • No persistent storage: Text submitted for AI processing is not stored on our servers after the response is returned
  • Secure API keys: Our API credentials are stored securely and never exposed client-side
  • Google Play security: All payment processing is handled by Google Play Billing — we never handle your payment information

While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please contact us at support@smarttypist.app.

♿
Section 5

Keyboard & Accessibility Service Usage

Critical Disclosure: Smart Typist operates as an Android Input Method Editor (IME). As a keyboard app, it technically has access to the text you type in the fields where it is active.

Here is our commitment regarding this access:

  • We only process text when you explicitly tap an AI button (Grammar Fix, Formal, Casual, etc.)
  • We never log, save, or transmit text typed in normal usage — only when you actively request AI enhancement
  • Smart Typist automatically disables AI features in sensitive fields like passwords, credit cards, and email addresses
  • AI processing is done on-demand; results are displayed to you and not retained on our servers
🤖
Section 6

AI Text Processing

When you use Smart Typist's AI features (Grammar Fix, Tone changes, Email mode, etc.), the text you select is sent to an AI service for processing. Here's how it works:

Request Flow

  • You type text and tap an AI mode button
  • Only the selected text is sent (not your entire chat history or context)
  • Text is encrypted and sent to our AI provider via HTTPS
  • The enhanced text is returned to your keyboard
  • The original text and result are not stored on any server

AI Provider

We use industry-standard AI APIs for text processing. These providers may have their own data retention policies. We use API settings that minimize data retention wherever possible. For sensitive use cases, we recommend reviewing the privacy practices of the AI provider.

Section 7

Third-Party Services

Smart Typist integrates with the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Billing: For subscription and purchase management — Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase (Google): Used for app analytics and crash reporting (anonymized) — Firebase Privacy Policy
  • AI API Provider: For processing text enhancement requests — data is minimized and not retained

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party providers for more information about their data practices.

Section 8

Children's Privacy

Smart Typist is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@smarttypist.app and we will promptly delete such information.

Section 9

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Withdraw consent: Revoke permissions you have previously granted

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@smarttypist.app. We will respond within 30 days.
